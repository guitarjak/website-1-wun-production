üìè Spacing & Layout

Padding & Margins

  • Section margins: 4rem bottom
  • Form group margin: 1.5rem bottom
  • Heading margin: 1rem bottom
  • Paragraph margin: 1rem bottom
  • Button padding: 0.75rem 1.5rem
  • Card padding: 1.5rem

Container Sizing

  • Max container width: 1280px (max-w-5xl equivalent)
  • Padding on sides: 1rem (4px)
  • Gap between elements: 1.5rem - 2rem

Border Radius

  • Default: 0.5rem (8px)
  • Cards: 0.75rem (12px)
  • Course cards: 1.5rem (24px)
  • Badges: 9999px (fully rounded)

‚ú® Animations

Transitions

  • Color transitions: 0.2s ease
  • All transitions: 0.3s ease
  • Progress bars: 0.3s ease

Hover Effects

  • Cards: Lift up 4px with enhanced shadow
  • Buttons: Lift up 2px with colored shadow
  • Links: Color changes (blue ‚Üí blue-dark)
